Sacred Nourishment

Divine Cuisine

Pure vegetarian, soul-nourishing fare prepared according to ancient Satvik traditions — food as a form of worship and a gift to the senses.

  • Govinda's Fine Dining — Michelin-starred Satvik cuisine with tasting menus inspired by Krishna's favourite childhood foods
  • Yamuna Roof Café — Open-air dining under the stars with Mathura pedha, masala chai, and monsoon-fresh seasonal specials
  • Prasad Hall — Traditional feast-style communal dining, served on banana leaves with 12-course Braj-style thali
  • Butter & Bliss Lounge — A tribute to Lord Krishna's love of makhan — artisan dairy desserts, live sweets-making, afternoon tiffin
  • Private in-suite dining by our personal butler, available 24 hours with customised ceremonial setup
